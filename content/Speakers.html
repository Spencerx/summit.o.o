Craig Gardner

Craig Gardner has enjoyed living the Linux Life for over 17 years. Starting with Slackware, Craig has used and promoted Linux in all its variants to overcome challenges for schools, small businesses, and large enterprises. Working for SUSE Craig has been instrumental in building and delivering dozens of Linux-based products, and has passionately promoted and implemented effective collaboration tools around the globe. As a community contributor, Craig builds MuseScore for SUSE, and helps in its development by providing nightly builds.

Andreas Jaeger

Andreas Jaeger has been contributing to Linux for over a decade. He ported the GNU C Library to x86-64 and lead the development effort to port Linux to the 64-bit x86-64 architecture. He had been responsible as project manager for the openSUSE distributions and is now part of the product management team at SUSE.
