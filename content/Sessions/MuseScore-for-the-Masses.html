MuseScore for the Masses

GARDNER, Craig - SUSE

PRESENTATION - Bonaire 6, Saturday, 1:15 p.m. to 2:00 p.m.

The musician in you is aching to find a way out into the open. You've got the killer riffs and catchy melodies in your head, but you need a way to capture the notes so that the rest of the band and the world can share your genius.

MuseScore is *THE* opensource music notation software. It runs on Linux, Mac and Windows. Not only is it the opensource alternative, its feature set rivals and in many ways exceeds that of costly proprietary products.

This presentation will expose you to all the great features of MuseScore, and paint the picture of its future.
