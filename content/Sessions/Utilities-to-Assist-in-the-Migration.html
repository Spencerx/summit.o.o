Utilities to Assist the Migration of Large Package Suites Between Linux Distributions


EGOLF, David - Bull Information Systems

PRESENTATION - Bonaire 7, Saturday, 3:15 p.m. to 4:00 p.m.


Many activities are required when migrating a large suite of packages from one distribution to another. We discuss several utilities that were developed to assist in performing these activities while migrating a large integrated suite of packages, which comprise Bull's HPC offering, from Red Hat® Enterprise Linux® (RHEL) to SUSE Linux Enterprise Server® (SLES). These utilities range from small ones that ease small tasks that are repeated many times a day to a large one that reduced a man-month task to a few hour automated run.
