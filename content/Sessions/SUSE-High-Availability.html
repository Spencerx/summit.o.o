SUSE High Availilty for mere mortals, a case study

Prof. DOUPNIK, Joe -Ingotec, MindworksUK, Univ of Oxford

PRESENTATION - Bonaire 6, Sunday, 10:15 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.

HA has the well deserved reputation of being challenging to learn and use. Yet it has marked benefits.

This talk describes how I climbed that learning curve to create a robust two node cluster with only local disks and a bevy of applications. The goal is to learn how HA works through this simple seeming situation as a preliminary step toward more advanced usage environments.
