Challenges Encountered During the Migration of a Large Integrated Software Suite From Red Hat® Enterprise Linux® to SUSE Linux Enterprise Server®

EGOLF, David - Bull Information Systems

Presentation - Bonaire 7, Friday, 3:00 p.m. to 3:45 p.m.

Many issues will be encountered when porting a large suite of packages from one distribution to another. We review a number of such issues that were encountered while migrating a large integrated suite of packages, which comprise Bull's HPC offering, from Red Hat® Enterprise Linux® (RHEL) to SUSE Linux Enterprise Server® (SLES). Alternative solutions are delineated, and the solutions that we selected are reviewed.
