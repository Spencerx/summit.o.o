ownCloud Geeko Games Poolside Dinner

The social event of the year begins at the Caribe Royale Hotel poolside brought to you by ownCloud, Your Cloud, Your Data, Your Way! It's our first night together and a great way to get to know your fellow Geekos from all over the world.

This poolside dinner party also brings to you an exclusive concert by openSUSE's very own Luis Nieto, Honduras's nationally-renowned musician and former director of the Honduras National Band. Prepare yourself for the soothing sounds of a saxophonist beloved by many in his country.

Then get ready to turn the night upside down as a frenzied pace ensues to build, decorate and race our boats across the pool. Boat kits will be provided and there's prizes for everyone. Better pack your swimsuit, because with a contest like this, you never know what is going to happen!

Cash Bar available on site.

The fun begins at 7:00 p.m. in a tent behind the Caribe Royale Hotel and the warm waters of a lake nearby. Satisfy your appetite with a delicious luau and toast your last night together with fellow Geekos before heading home on Sunday.
