Summit Exclusive: DVD Signing Party

5 September, 2012


Today, the openSUSE Summit team joins the global community in celebrating the long-anticipated official release of openSUSE 12.2.

We're so estatic, we can't help ourselves. So, we're announcing exclusives you'll only get when you come to the openSUSE Summit, just 16 days from now. Join SUSE Vice-President Michael Miller and openSUSE Board member, Andrew Wafaa, co-hosts of the Summit opening ceremony at 5 p.m. on Friday, September 21. They'll take you on an openSUSE Journey Around the World.

After the ceremony, you'll get something no one else can have. Bragging rights! Be the first in North America to get your hands on a shiny freshly-minted copy of the openSUSE 12.2 DVD. That's right. Everybody else will have to wait just a little bit longer to get their hands on a DVD, but not you.

And to sweeten the pot, the opening ceremony is also a DVD signing party! Miller and Wafaa will hang around to sign your DVD, making this your very own personalized copy of openSUSE 12.2. We just upped your bragging rights, didn't we?

Of course, if you're dying to get some cool green 12.2 on your computer, there's no need to wait. You can download the electronic version of openSUSE 12.2 today and still come to the Summit to pick up the shiny version. So stay tuned because this is just the beginning of exciting exclusives brought to you by the openSUSE Summit Team!
