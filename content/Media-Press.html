Press room

The openSUSE Summit will feature a press room suitable for interviews. Please let us know if you would like to make use of this facility.

Press conference

The openSUSE Summit will provide a press conference with the keynote speakers as well as people from the openSUSE Board. This is tentatively schedule for Friday, Sept 21, after the keynote, 14:00-14:30.
