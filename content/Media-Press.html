Press room

The openSUSE Conference will feature a press room suitable for interviews. Please let us know if you would like to make use of this facility.

Press conference

At the openSUSE Summit a press conference will be held to allow questioning the keynote speakers as well as people from the openSUSE Board. The preliminairy time and date for the press conference is Friday, Sept 21, after the keynote, 14:00-14:30.
