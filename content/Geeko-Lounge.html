Geeko Lounge



Geekos are a hard-working, fun-loving bunch. But even Geekos need a break to socialize and get some food in their bellies. That's why we bring you the Geeko Lounge. A place to relax, put your feet up and hang out with fellow Geekos.


The Geeko Lounge will remain open throughout the weekend. Each morning, beginning at 8:00 a.m., a Continental Breakfast will be served. Throughout the day, we'll have refreshments to quench your thirst and nibble on. Couches and bean bags to plop your dupa on as you take a breather from the hustle and bustle of the busy day checking out sessions.


And, its also a place to meet our sponsors and other vendors. You'll meet folks from Omnibond, ownCloud, openSUSE Forums, local LUG groups, and many more. It's also the place where you can grab a bunch of materials like brochures and DVDs to take home and promote openSUSE in your neighborhood.
