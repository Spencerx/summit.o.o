The openSUSE Summit (#oSSum13) brings together a wide variety of Open Source contributors to collaborate on one of the major Linux distribution projects. In three exciting days of workshops and social happenings we bring alive our slogan "Have a lot of fun". oSSum13 takes place November 15-17, 2013 following SUSEcon , our main sponsors' yearly conference. Registrations finalized before November 1st include free admission to Technology Showcase and Keynotes of SUSECon on Friday November 15.

Program

The oSSum13 program will include keynotes, talks, workshops and a lot of fun.

Social Events

Geekos are a hard-working, fun-loving bunch. But even Geekos need a break to socialize and get some food in their bellies. That's why we bring you the Geeko unge. A place to relax, put your feet up and hang out with fellow Geekos. Each morning we serve breakfast, throughout the day, we'll have refreshments to quench your thirst and nibble on. Couches and bean bags to plop your dupa on as you take a breather from the hustle and bustle of the busy day checking out sessions. We are also planning to add a party or two, thus check back to get the latest info.

Location

The openSUSE Summit will be held November 15-17, 2013 in beautiful sunny (we hope!) Lake Buena Vista just outside of Orlando, Florida. Disney's Coronado Springs Resort will welcome all Geekos to this years event. At the Disney Resort you are located right next door to one of the greatest attractions in Orlando. But Orlando has more to offer than Disney World, it is also home to Sea World, Universal Studios and the Kenedy Space center is only abou 60 miles away. If you are interested in the various attractions you might want to check out an Orlando city pass.
