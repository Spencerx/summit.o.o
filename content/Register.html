Register for the openSUSE Summit!

The openSUSE Summit is an entirely free event. As there are no tickets or limitations on entry, you can just show up. However it is highly appreciated if you register. If you register, we promise you:

  • To make sure you'll get a t-shirt and possibly other goodies
  • To make sure we have food and drinks for you (restaurants are NOT close by!)
  • To make sure we have room in the sessions for you!

Please note: We have a Code of Conduct for this conference and by registering and attending you declare to be aware off and comply with it!
