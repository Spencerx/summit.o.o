Location

The openSUSE Summit will be held November 15-17, 2013 in beautiful sunny (we hope!) Lake Buena Vista just outside of Orlando, Florida. Disney's Coronado Springs Resort will welcome all Geekos to this years event.

Airport Shuttle:

Disney offers complimentary Bus transportation to and from Orlando International Airport. You will need to reserve your seat on the bus accoring to the published guidelines. If you would rather not ride on the "Magical Express, but who would want to miss that experience there is also a way to get to the resort using public transportation.

About Orlando:

The Summit takes place at the Disney Resort an dthus you are located right next door to one of the greatest attractions in Orlando. But Orlando has more to offer than Disney World, it is also home to Sea World and Universal Studios. If you are interested in the various attractions you might want to check out an Orlando city pass.

The Kenedy Space center is only abou 60 miles away and also a great place to visit.
